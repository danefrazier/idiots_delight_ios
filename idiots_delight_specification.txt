Idiot's Delight — iOS App Specification
=========================================
Date: 2026-04-16
Version: 2.0 (reflects shipped implementation)

1. Overview
-----------
Idiot's Delight is a single-player solitaire card game for iPhone (and
iPad) built natively in Swift / SwiftUI. The player removes cards from
four stacks according to suit-based rules and wins by leaving exactly
one ace in each stack.

2. Deck
-------
- Standard 52-card deck; no jokers.
- Suits: Spades, Clubs, Hearts, Diamonds.
- Values (low to high): 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, Jack, Queen, King, Ace.
- Aces are HIGH (numeric value 14, above King=13).
- Deck is shuffled three times before the first deal.

3. Setup
--------
- Four stacks, all initially empty.
- Player taps "Deal Next Round" to begin.
- All cards are always face-up.

4. Game Structure
-----------------
The game proceeds in up to 13 rounds (52 cards / 4 per round).
Each round: one card is dealt from the deck to each of the four stacks.
The player performs remove / ace-move actions in any order, then taps
"Deal Next Round" when ready.

5. Player Actions
-----------------
Remove a card
  Player taps a non-ace stack top card.
  Legal when ALL of the following are true:
    i.   Stack is not empty.
    ii.  Top card is not an ace.
    iii. At least one other stack's top card shares the same suit.
    iv.  At least one other showing card of the same suit has a higher
         numeric value (relaxed rule: any lower card may be removed, not
         just the strict lowest — see section 6).

Move an ace
  Player taps a stack whose top card is an ace → stack enters
  "selected" (highlighted) state.
  Player then taps an empty stack → ace moves there.
  Tapping the selected stack again deselects it.
  Aces may only be moved to empty stacks.

Deal next round
  Player taps "Deal Next Round". Button is disabled when deck is empty
  or game is over.

New Game
  "New Game" button resets the full game state and reshuffles the deck.

6. Removal Rule — Detail
------------------------
A card X may be removed if and only if:
  - X is not an ace, AND
  - At least one other visible top card shares X's suit, AND
  - At least one other visible card of that suit has a strictly higher
    numeric value than X.

This is a relaxed interpretation: the player may remove any non-ace
showing card as long as a higher same-suit card is also showing. The
player is NOT restricted to removing only the strict lowest of the suit.

Advisory (hint mode only): if a lower same-suit card is also showing,
the status area displays a message suggesting the player consider the
lower card instead.

7. Win Condition
----------------
All four stacks contain exactly one card and that card is an ace (any
suit). Aces need not be of different suits. Status message shown
immediately; win overlay fades in after a 2-second delay.

8. Loss Conditions
------------------
a. Deck runs out during "Deal Next Round" — status message shown
   immediately; game over overlay appears after a 3-second delay.
b. No legal moves remain AND the deck is empty — detected automatically
   after any action or deal; status message shown immediately, overlay
   after 3-second delay.

If no legal moves remain but the deck still has cards, the game waits
silently; the player must deal the next round.

9. Ace Killer Detection
-----------------------
An "Ace Killer" occurs when a King lands directly on top of a same-suit
Ace (e.g., King of Hearts on Ace of Hearts). The Ace is permanently
buried — it can never be removed — making the win condition impossible
to achieve for that suit stack.

When an Ace Killer is detected:
  - aceKillerCount stat is recorded immediately and synchronously.
  - After a 2-second delay (to let the king card render), a transparent
    overlay alert appears naming the suit.
  - The player may choose "Keep Playing" or "Start Over".
  - A crown badge appears on the affected stack column.

10. Hint Mode
-------------
A toggle switch in the header enables Hint Mode. When active:
  - The best stack to tap is highlighted with a pulsing yellow glow
    (1.6 s ease-in-out, repeating).
  - Priority: optimal move (no advisory) > any legal move.
  - Exception: if the only available move is to relocate an ace that is
    alone in its stack, no hint is shown (the destination choice is
    positional and up to the player).
  - If a sub-optimal removal is available (a lower same-suit card
    exists), the status message describes the advisory.
  - Turning off hint mode stops the glow immediately (0.2 s ease-out).

11. Game Statistics
-------------------
Tracked across all sessions (persisted via UserDefaults):
  - Total games played
  - Total wins / win percentage
  - Average cards remaining on loss
  - Ace Killers Encountered (incremented at detection time)

A Stats sheet (bar-chart icon in header) displays all statistics and
offers a "Reset Stats" action with a two-button confirmation
("No, Keep My Stats" / "Yes, Reset Everything — destructive role).

12. Orientation Support
-----------------------
Persistent header bar (both orientations)
  Single top row: "Idiot's Delight" title · Hints toggle · Stats ·
  About. Separated from the layout below by a subtle divider.

Portrait
  Stacked VStack layout below the header. Card size 75×105 pt.
  Status message below stacks. "Deal Next Round" button below message.
  "New Game" button at bottom.

Landscape (verticalSizeClass == .compact)
  Three-column HStack layout below the header.
  Left panel (110 pt): round/deck info, status message, New Game.
  Center: four stack columns sized dynamically via GeometryReader —
    card width = floor((available - safe insets - panels - gaps - padding
    - card gaps) / 4), clamped to 82% of safe height, 1:1.4 ratio.
    Safe area insets (notch/Dynamic Island) subtracted before sizing.
  Right panel (130 pt): Deal Next Round button, centred vertically.

13. About Screen
----------------
Sheet presented from the info-circle button in the header.
Contains:
  - Joker card illustration (transparent PNG, max 260 pt wide).
  - "Idiot's Delight" title.
  - "Developed by D. Frazier" credit line.
  - Cabin story: "This game was taught by family friends while at a
    cabin in the mountains on a weekend trip in the 1980s. This is an
    homage to good times, and good friends."
Background matches game green (0.1, 0.35, 0.14).

---------------------------

    AceKillerOverlay.swift — Full-screen modal for Ace Killer alert
    StatsView.swift        — Statistics sheet
    AboutView.swift        — About sheet with joker image
  Assets.xcassets/
    AppIcon.appiconset/
    Joker.imageset/        — joker.png (transparent RGBA PNG, 1024×1036)

15. Technical Notes
-------------------
- Swift 6, Xcode 26, iOS 26+ target.
- SWIFT_DEFAULT_ACTOR_ISOLATION = MainActor,
  SWIFT_APPROACHABLE_CONCURRENCY = YES.
- @Observable macro used throughout (not ObservableObject/@Published).
- @Bindable for two-way bindings into GameState from GameView.
- PBXFileSystemSynchronizedRootGroup: all Swift files auto-discovered;
  no manual "Add Files to Xcode" step required.
- Hint glow driven by @State pulsing + withAnimation(.repeatForever);
  stopped via .onChange(of: isHinted) with explicit animation block.
- EmptyStackView uses .contentShape(Rectangle()) for tap detection on
  transparent interior.
- Joker image background removed via Pillow edge flood-fill (grey ~106
  RGB, tolerance 45) to produce transparent PNG.
- Stats persisted under UserDefaults key "idiotsDelight.gameStats".
- Simulator target: iPhone 17, iOS 26.4 (0322476A-BB89-417A-8131-3E3141119435).

17. Known Limitations / Future Work
-------------------------------------
- No undo functionality.
- No card deal animation (slide-in per stack).
- No confetti / win animation.
- No iPad-optimised split-view layout.
- No iCloud sync for stats across devices.
