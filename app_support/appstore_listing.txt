A Classic Solitaire Card Game

DESCRIPTION
-----------
Idiot's Delight is a classic solitaire card game passed down through
generations of card players. Simple to learn, surprisingly strategic —
can you clear the deck down to four aces?

Deal four cards at a time across four stacks. Remove cards when a
higher card of the same suit is showing. Move aces to empty stacks to
keep your options open. Win by leaving exactly one ace on each stack.

FEATURES
• Hint mode — highlights your best available move with a pulsing glow
• Ace Killer alerts — warns you when a King buries a same-suit Ace,
  making the game unwinnable
• Game statistics — tracks wins, losses, win percentage, and Ace
  Killer incidents
• Portrait and landscape support
• No ads, no in-app purchases, no account required

A personal homage — this game was taught by family friends at a cabin
in the mountains in the 1980s. Good times, good friends.
