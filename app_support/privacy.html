Idiot's Delight

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

Overview

Idiot's Delight is a single-player card game developed by D. Frazier. This policy describes how the app handles your information. The short version: it doesn't collect any.

Data We Do Not Collect

Idiot's Delight does not collect, transmit, share, or sell any personal data. Specifically, the app does not access or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
  • Health or financial data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores game statistics (wins, losses, and related counters) locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults mechanism. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

You can reset this data at any time from within the app: tap the bar chart icon → Reset Stats.

Third-Party Services

Idiot's Delight does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. It is appropriate for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
