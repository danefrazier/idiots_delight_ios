About the Game

Idiot's Delight is a classic solitaire card game. Deal four cards at a time, remove cards when a higher card of the same suit is showing, and win by leaving exactly one ace on each stack. Taught by family friends at a cabin in the mountains in the 1980s — an homage to good times and good friends.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I move an ace?
Tap the ace to select it (it will highlight), then tap an empty stack to move it there.


What is an Ace Killer?
When a King of the same suit lands directly on top of an Ace, that Ace is permanently buried. The game alerts you immediately so you can start over if you wish.


What does Hint Mode do?
Toggle the Hints switch in the header. The best available move will pulse with a yellow glow.


How do I reset my stats?
Tap the bar chart icon → scroll to the bottom → Reset Stats.
